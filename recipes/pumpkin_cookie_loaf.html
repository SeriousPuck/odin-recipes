Pumpkin Cookie Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 large can of pumpkin pie mix
  • 1 bag betty crocker chocolate-chip cookie dough mix
  • walnuts, chopped

Steps

  1. in a large bowl, mix the can of pumpkin pie mix and the cookie dough mix
  2. mix in walnuts to mixture
  3. pour the mixture into a 8x13 baking pan
  4. pre-heat an oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Add the pan to the oven, and bake for 30-45 minutes
  6. remove from oven and let cool, then enjoy
