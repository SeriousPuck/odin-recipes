Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 4 large potatoes, cubed
  • 4 large carrots, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 package dry onion soup mix
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. place meat and flour in a plastic bag, shake to coat meat.
  2. heat large skillet with olive oil and brown meat in batches until evenly browned.
  3. place browned meat into a skillet
  4. in the same skillet add the butter and the saute the onions until soft
  5. deglaze the pan with two cups of water and pour into slow cooker
  6. add the potatoes, carrots, parsely, salte and pepper into the slow cooker
  7. mix in the dry soup mix into the slow cooker
  8. Cover and cook on high for 30 minutes
  9. if you want it to be thicker make a slurry with flour and some water and mix into slow cooker
  10. simmer until desireed thickness is reached
