Pasta Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of pasta (rotini, ziti, or penne are preferred)
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 small diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 jar (28 ounces) of tomato-based pasta sauce
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit (230 degrees Celsius)
  2. In a large pot cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook the ground beef in a medium skillet until brown. Remove ground beef and saute onions until soft.
  4. Combine cooked beef, noodles, onion, all the seasonings and the pasta sauce together.
  5. Add the mixture to a baking pan and cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Place pan in pre-heated oven and bake for 20 minutes covered.
  7. Remove foil and sprinkle more cheese and bake another 5-10 minutes.
  8. Serve Warm
